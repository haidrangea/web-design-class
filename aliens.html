This is a GRAVE MISTAKE that is known to have led to the deaths of many a sorcerer. If you cannot determine whether or not you are powerful enough, or have acquired a completely new species of alien, DO NOT LET IT OUT. Below, a list of known alien species can be found:

Pfhor: They are bipedal, taller than humans, have three red eyes in a triangle formation, and gray skin. They are known to follow some sort of religion, and have a very complex bureaucracy. They are well known for enslaving humans.

Grays: These aliens are short, with very spindly limbs, gigantic heads, big black eyes, and gray skin. They are known for using the "flying saucer" in order to traverse the cosmos. They frequently abduct cows and humans in order to perform experiments related to the development of baby humans, how baby humans are raised, the effect of milk on human babies, and the human concept of "consciousness".

Nordle: Greatly resemble humans, especially those from the region on Earth known as "Scandinavia". In fact, the race of Humans are descended from Nordles, who arrived in northern europe from the star Aldebaran around eight thousand years ago. They came into contact with Great Ape tribes, which led to the race of Humans we have today. Nordles are very tall, powerful, motivated, driven, honest, and honorable, and are known to greatly venerate their ancestors.

Oa: The Oa are a race of floating slime balls. When asexually concepted, they appear as a plume of teal liquid underwater near the shore. This eventually forms into a ball of slime that will float off of the ground. This ball has a large eye, allowing the Oa to see, and two to four "orbitals" of stickier slime that is connected to the main body with a white tendril, allowing the Oa to use the "orbitals" as limbs. The Oa is always connected to its birthplace with a telephone cord-like tendril that feeds it water passively.
