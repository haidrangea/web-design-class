Alien Language

It has been determined by hermeticist Ricardo Chandler that all aliens speak the same language. This language has been determined to be quite difficul to decode, using many sounds found impossible for the human vocal chords to produce. These sounds include the "xh" sound, the glottal stop "pxch", and the interjection "xoombawxamxchtha", frequently shortened to "xchtha". Below, you may find a helpful video of Alien language, as well as a list of some common Alien words.

  • Oxth: Mother
  • Owthx: Father
  • Kxa: Cantankerous
  • X: The
  • Darra: Tractor Beam
  • Poxxop: UFO
  • Zaxdx: Salubrious
